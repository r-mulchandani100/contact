Speaker Series

Listen to professionals and get access to new experiences!

Summary

One of the most influential and informative events that LEFA hosts are its Speaker Series. Every month, professionals within the fields of business, economics, and finance, including college graduates from Ivy Leagues, professors who have taught for decades, and leaders of multinational companies, come to talk about their knowledge and experiences. They then host Question and Answer seminars to help pioneering students better understand how the world of economics works. LEFA’s Speaker Series is the flagship of its education initiative, and LEFA continues to strive to make this event the best education experience on the East Coast.
